My Coding Journey

I started my coding journey in March 2022 shortly after receiving my A Level results. As I was researching on prospective courses offered by universities, I stumbled across a Youtube coding video by Dani which inspired me to pick up coding. I was fascinated by the intrinsic nature of coding and its innate ability and freedom to create applications bounded solely by our own creativity and skillsets. I then decided to tryout coding for a few weeks which I slowly realised that I would deeply enjoy pursuing a career in software development. Subsequently, I have been ultilising my free time in National Service to learn various programming languages and skills such as web development. Eventually, I hope that I would be able reach my goal of deploying meaningful quality applications that can create a positive impact in Society.
